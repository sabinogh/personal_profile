International Sales

Project Overview

Analyze and manage not only what was sold, but the manager has initial control over what was negotiated, and the payment methods and has knowledge of how it will impact the company's cash flow.

Note: It is not the Sales Manager's objective to create or analyze the cash flow, but he/she has objectives and goals in the negotiations to avoid any problems that are aligned with the Financial Manager and Board of Directors.

Sales Dashboard

This dashboard provides a broad view of how much was sold by customers and how much will be received monthly. It can be filtered by type of sale such as Parts, Services (Field), and Services (Factory) along with the location on the map describing where the sale was made by country.

You can also quickly check the progress of sales, such as Total Sold, Amount Received, Amount Overdue, and Amount Receivable.

Receipt

For a better view of receipts according to what was negotiated, they are separated by type of client, country, and amount per month.

Sales 23 and 24

Here we have a visualization of sales comparison between the years 2023 and 2024. It contains all the values ​​in an analytical way by the table and in a bar graph showing this comparison in a visual way.

The card shows that although the analysis was with data only up to July 2024, the result is positive compared to the previous year.
